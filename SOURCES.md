**Reliability Assessment**: Sources are included representing multiple perspectives:

- ✅ **High Reliability**: Peer-reviewed academic sources, government publications, geological surveys
- ⚠️ **Medium Reliability**: Mainstream journalism, established publishers, documented historical accounts
